Breast Cancer

Gestational infections

Gestational infections refer to infections that occur during pregnancy and can potentially affect both the mother and the developing fetus. While pregnancy is a time of great joy and anticipation, it's essential to be aware of the risks associated with gestational infections.

Anemia

Anemia is a common health condition characterized by a decrease in the number of red blood cells or a deficiency in hemoglobin, the protein responsible for transporting oxygen in the bloodstream. It affects millions of people worldwide and can have a significant impact on overall well-being.
